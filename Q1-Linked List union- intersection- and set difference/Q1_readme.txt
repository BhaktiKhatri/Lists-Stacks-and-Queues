Run LinkedListSetOperations.java file.
It will ask for size of both the Linked List and their elements. Enter your values.
Example:

Enter the size of the first Linked Lists: 
6
Enter elements for the first Linked List: 
2
4
5
7
10
12
Enter the size of the second Linked Lists: 
5
Enter elements for the second Linked List: 
1
3
4
7
9
List1: 2 4 5 7 10 12 
List2: 1 3 4 7 9 
-----Intersect Operation-----

Intersect Output List: 4 7 
-----Union Operation-----

Union Output List: 1 2 3 4 5 7 9 10 12 
-----Set Difference Operation-----

Difference Output List: 2 5 10 12
